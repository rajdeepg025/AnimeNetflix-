Chainsaw Man follows Denji, a poor young man struggling to survive while working as a devil hunter to pay off his father’s debt. His only companion is Pochita, a small chainsaw devil who helps him hunt other devils. After being betrayed and killed, Denji is revived through a contract with Pochita, becoming Chainsaw Man—a human with the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws. He is then taken in by the Public Safety Devil Hunters, where he begins working under the mysterious Makima. As Denji fights powerful devils, he experiences a new life filled with danger, strange relationships, and simple dreams like living comfortably and being loved. The story mixes brutal action, dark humor, and emotional depth, making it unique and unpredictable.
