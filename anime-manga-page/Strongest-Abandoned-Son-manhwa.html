Strongest Abandoned Son

⭐ 7.5 🟢 Ongoing

Strongest Abandoned Son follows Ye Mo, a powerful cultivator from another world who suddenly finds himself reincarnated on modern Earth—where spiritual energy is extremely scarce. Not only has he lost his former strength, but he also wakes up in the body of a young man who has been abandoned and humiliated by his own family. Determined to rise again, Ye Mo begins cultivating from scratch while navigating a modern world filled with hidden dangers, powerful enemies, and ruthless rivals. As he regains his strength, he uncovers secrets about his past life and builds his path toward becoming powerful once more. With intense martial arts, cultivation progression, and a classic “revenge + comeback” storyline, the series delivers a satisfying journey of growth and dominance.
