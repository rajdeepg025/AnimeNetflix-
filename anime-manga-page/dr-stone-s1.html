Dr Stone

S1 Ep12

Dr. Stone follows Senku Ishigami, a brilliant young scientist who awakens thousands of years after a mysterious event turns all of humanity into stone. In this new world where civilization has completely collapsed, Senku is determined to rebuild society using the power of science. Teaming up with his strong and loyal friend Taiju Oki, Senku begins the journey of recreating technology from scratch—starting from basic tools to advanced inventions. As they progress, they encounter new allies and rivals who have different visions for the future of humanity. Facing challenges from nature and opposing forces like Tsukasa Shishio, Senku uses knowledge, creativity, and experimentation to overcome obstacles and push humanity forward.
