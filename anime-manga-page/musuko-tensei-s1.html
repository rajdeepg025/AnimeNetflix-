Musuko Tensei

S1 Ep25

Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation follows a man who dies in his old life full of regrets and is reborn in a magical world as Rudeus Greyrat. Unlike a normal child, he keeps all memories from his past life and decides to live this new life seriously, without repeating his mistakes. From a young age, Rudeus shows incredible talent in magic and begins learning under skilled mentors. As he grows, his journey takes him across dangerous lands, where he meets powerful allies like Eris Boreas Greyrat and Roxy Migurdia. After a mysterious disaster teleports people across the world, Rudeus sets out on a long journey to reunite with his family. Along the way, he faces deadly creatures, emotional struggles, and life-changing challenges that shape him into a stronger and more mature person. The story blends adventure, magic, and deep character growth, showing how one person tries to build a better life from zero.
