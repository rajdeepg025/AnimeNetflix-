Mushoku Tensei

⭐ 8.5 🟢 Ongoing

Mushoku Tensei follows a jobless and hopeless man who gets a second chance at life after being reincarnated in a magical world as Rudeus Greyrat. Determined not to repeat his past mistakes, he decides to live this new life to the fullest. Gifted with strong magical talent from a young age, Rudeus begins training and exploring the world, meeting new friends, powerful warriors, and skilled mages along the way. As he grows, he faces challenges, personal struggles, and life-changing adventures that shape his journey. Blending fantasy, adventure, and character growth, the story focuses on redemption, learning from past mistakes, and becoming a better person.
