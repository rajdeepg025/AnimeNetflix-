Attack On Titan

S1 Ep12

Attack on Titan Season 1 follows Eren Yeager, a young boy living in a world where humanity is forced to hide behind massive walls to survive against giant man-eating creatures known as Titans. His life changes forever when a colossal Titan breaches the outer wall, bringing destruction and tragedy. Driven by anger and a desire for revenge, Eren joins the military along with his friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert. As they train and fight against the Titans, they witness the harsh reality of survival and the heavy cost of war. As the battle for humanity continues, unexpected secrets about the Titans and Eren himself begin to emerge, changing everything they thought they knew. The story blends intense action, mystery, and emotional struggles as humanity fights to take back its freedom.
