Jujutsu Kaisen

S1 Ep24

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a physically strong high school student whose life changes when he encounters a cursed object—one of the fingers of a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna. After swallowing it to save his friends, Yuji becomes the host of Sukuna, a dangerous and ancient curse. He is then introduced to the world of Jujutsu Sorcerers, who fight against curses—evil manifestations born from human negative emotions. Under the guidance of Satoru Gojo, Yuji joins Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he trains alongside other sorcerers to control his powers and battle deadly enemies. As the story unfolds, Yuji faces intense battles, powerful curses, and moral challenges, all while trying to protect innocent lives. The anime blends action, dark themes, and emotional moments, making it a thrilling experience. Why You Should Watch: It is known for its insane fight animation, strong characters, and dark yet engaging storyline that keeps you hooked from the very first episode.
