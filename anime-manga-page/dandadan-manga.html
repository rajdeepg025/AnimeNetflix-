Dandadan follows Momo Ayase, a high school girl who believes in ghosts, and Ken Takakura (nicknamed Okarun), who strongly believes in aliens. After a heated argument to prove each other wrong, they each encounter something shocking—Momo faces real spirits, while Okarun comes across extraterrestrial beings. Their lives quickly spiral into chaos as they get caught between supernatural forces and alien threats. Gaining strange powers along the way, the duo teams up to fight bizarre enemies, uncover mysteries, and survive increasingly dangerous encounters. Blending fast-paced action, comedy, romance, and the supernatural, Dandadan delivers a wild and unpredictable story that keeps readers hooked every chapter.
