The Tutorial Tower Of The Advanced Player follows Kim Hyeonu, a hunter who gets mysteriously trapped inside a deadly tutorial tower filled with endlessly regenerating monsters. For 12 long years, he is forced to survive alone, constantly fighting and growing stronger. When he finally escapes, he is no longer the same person—he has become overwhelmingly powerful, far beyond normal hunters. Returning to the real world, Hyeonu sets out to uncover the truth behind his imprisonment while facing new enemies, hidden conspiracies, and powerful threats. With nonstop action, overpowered main character moments, and a mix of mystery and revenge, the story delivers a fast-paced and satisfying experience as Hyeonu dominates everything in his path.
